Investigation and Planning

While reading the brief the quote “Data is the new oil. Like oil, data is valuable, but if unrefined it cannot really be used.” Clive Humby became increasingly important and it became clear that the data set I chose had to be useful to me in some way once processed otherwise it would be entirely pointless. While thinking about the possible data sets I could use 2 major ones that came to mind

  1. House Prices Over Time by Area – This would help me analyze trends in housing markets, allowing for more informed decisions when planning a future move.
  2. Salary Influencing Factors (Age, Education, Experience, etc.) – This dataset could be useful for understanding income trends and how different variables impact salary growth.

After evaluating both options, I decided on the salary dataset because it is important to the public to know what factors influence their salary.

We had 12 weeks to complete this project so I decided to use a gantt chart to make sure I stuck to a strict timeline to ensure I didn't get side tracked and spend too much time on certain parts of the project.
