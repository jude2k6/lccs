Controls

  • Graph Modes

    In the top left corner click on the different graph modes; Zoom, Pan

  • Zoom

    If the plot's drag mode is set to 'Zoom', click and drag on the plot to zoom-in and double-click to zoom-out completely. It will autoscale both axis

  • Pan

    If the plot's drag mode is set to 'Pan', click and drag on the plot to pan and double-click to reset the pan.

  • Filter

    On the legend located on the left of the graphs, click on the different items to filter them out.
